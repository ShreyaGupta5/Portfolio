RF Transmission Drone Hardware Project

Designed and assembled a hardware-based RF-controlled drone using transmitter-receiver modules and KK 2.1.5 flight controller for wireless control, calibration, and stable flight testing.

  • RF signal transmission and reception using wireless transmitter-receiver modules
  • Motor, ESC, flight controller, and frame-level hardware integration
  • KK 2.1.5 flight controller calibration and flight stability tuning
  • Circuit debugging, wiring validation, testing, and iterative hardware improvement
